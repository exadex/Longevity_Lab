Scientific references & data sources

This platform is based on a combination of in vitro and in vivo literature describing key biological processes involved in tissue aging, including vascular function, extracellular matrix remodeling, inflammation, and cellular communication.

Additional references have been integrated from vascular biology and aging research to complement classical hallmarks of aging (López-Otín et al.), particularly for pathways not explicitly covered in the original framework.

The aim is to provide a multi-layered interface for both non-specialist users and research-oriented users.

Score calculation method

1. Selection of 24 key biomarkers measured in ExAdEx tissue models (transcriptomic and secretomic data).
2. Comparison to age-matched reference datasets.
3. Weighted integration into a composite aging score.
